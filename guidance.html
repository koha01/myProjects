Guidance for Agios Athanasios

Get extra info about what to pack and how to get to Agios Athanasios

What to pack


In general, whilst at Agios Athanasios
you're going to need the following items:
  • 2 pairs of socks
  • Gloves
  • Big backpack
  • Ski jacket and pants
  • Base and Mid Layers
  • Sunscreen
  • Neckwarmer

How to get to Agios Athanasios


In case you're not joining the team on the
bus ride, you can easily find the
directions for Agios Athanasios over here!
Finally, be careful when it comes to
the road leading up to the mountain.
Because of the low temperatures and
weather, you need to make sure that your
car is supplied with snow tyres or chains.
