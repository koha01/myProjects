Activities at Agios Athanasios

Learn about what you can do at Agios Athanasios

Restaurants

Agios Athanasios offers its visitors, a plethora
of restaurants in which they can enjoy a variety of
traditional and delicious food, whilst being affordable.
Finally, you can check these restaurants for yourself and
decide, which one of them you'd like to visit from now!

Night Life

Even though one might say that Agios Athanasios is just
a small village, there's a surprisingly great amount of
bars and clubs in which visitors can have loads
of fun with friends!

Agios Athanasios Outdoor Activities

Beyond skiing and the activities mentioned above,
visitors of Agios Athanasios can partake in numerous
outdoor activities. Some of those could be:

  • Horse Riding
  • Hunting
  • Hiking
  • Snowmobiling
  • ...and a lot others
