Ski Friday is here and the Agios Athanasios Skiers have decided to give all skiers a chance to get several items at crazy low prices! Check it out!



New Season Starting


12/11/2022

The 2022 season has officially started! We've been informed by the Agios Athanasios resort officials, that the 3 last days loads of snow has covered the resort, leading to it having a 6 inch layer of fresh powder. Additionally, we've learned that 7 out of the 10 pists and 5 out of the 6 ski lifts are going to be operational!



Summer Training Season Official Start


20/06/2022

Get ready skiers, this whole summer we will be getting our bodies ready and all trained up, in order to be able to visit Agios Athanasios during the 2022 winter season.
